Team

Coming from various expertise and background,
We believe in the power of diversity.

Yu-Po Hua

Founder & Consultant

YU-PO HUA is a researcher and consultant in social innovation and sustainability. She has 5+ years of research and practice on social entrepreneurship, social innovation, and circular economy. She was selected as Circular Economy Pioneer in 2021 by the Ellen MacArthur Foundation. Her entrepreneurial projects were recognized as "Outstanding Sustainable Entrepreneurship” by Impact Hub Taipei and received the "Social Business Award" by Yunus Center Taiwan.

Yu-Po holds an Erasmus International Master in Global Market & Local Creativities (awarded by University of Glasgow, Göttingen, and Barcelona), and another MA in National Development from National Taiwan University. Her previous experiences working with various NGOs and international volunteering led to social entrepreneurship. She founded Infinite Value in 2020 in search of solutions to global issues.

Chun-Hsiang Chan

Data Scientist & Consultant

CHUN-HSIANG CHAN is a data scientist. He received his PhD. in Geography from the National Taiwan University. His expertise is on issues related to Spatio-temporal, geographical changes, complex network science, infectious disease communication, and social network media, etc. He holds professional skills in GIS (ArcGIS, QGIS, Super GIS), Spatial Statistics, Complex Network Analysis, Gephi, Python, C#, JavaScript, and jQueryUI.

Chun-Hsiang researches the features and changes of sustainable development through data science and provides data-driven policy as the reference basis and recommendations for the government's sustainable development. Several of his researches and papers were published in SCI and SSCI journals. Currently, he works as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence, Tamkang University.

Xuan-Zhen Chen

Consultant in Health & Medicine

XUAN-ZHEN CHEN is a certified pharmacist. He has 5+ years of work experience from Taipei Tzu Chi Hospital, iHealth (a social enterprise in healthcare), and long-term care institutions. He holds expert in medical knowledge, health behavior promotion, and home care services.

Xuan-Zhen received his bachelor's degree from the Department of Pharmacy, National Taiwan University(NTU). Due to his research interest, he is now enrolled in the master's program of the Institute of Health Behaviors and Community Science in NTU.

Si-Jie Zhang

Consultant in Forestry & Organic Farming

SI-JIE ZHANG has 5+ years of work experience in forestry research, biotechnology production management, and quality control. Her expertise is production planning, data collection, experiment design, tissue and seedling domestication, and crop cultivation.

Si-Jie received her bachelor's degree from the Department of Forestry and Natural Resources, National Chiayi University. She is currently working in the field of organic farming.
