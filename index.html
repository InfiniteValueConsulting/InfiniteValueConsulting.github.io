The Story Behind

INFINITE VALUE was founded in 2020. It all started with a case study that made our founder reflect on the meaning of “sustainability” and the current dominant angles in the field.She acknowledged the need for more discourses and localized solutions. Meanwhile, many friends working in social enterprises and NGOs faced operational challenges during the corona crisis. To support them, she started to work on pro-bono consulting cases and strategic planning.These incidents led to the insight, "Why not start a social enterprise that supports local businesses, communities, and people to implement sustainable strategies and generate social impact?"

Our Vision

We place social impact and environmental sustainability at the center. As we believe that knowledge means more responsibility towards society, we support local businesses, communities, and groups to apply sustainable strategies. To contribute to a better world, we drive the changes together.

Our Expertise

Coming from various expertise and background, we believe in the power of diversity and experiment with different approaches. We also involve and collaborate with experts from different fields and organizations. Meaning that in each project, we find the right person and methods to tackle them.

Our Project

To implement sustainable strategies and generate social impact, we practice in different fields and projects, from social entrepreneurship, social innovation, circular economy, SDGs, rural development, community building, education, and art creation.

Our Services

We support businesses, communities and local groups through various methods
to implement sustainability and generate social impacts in our society.

  • Consulting

    We provide consulting services to improve the organization's performance and implement sustainability.

  • Strategic Planning

    We provide strategies for implementing circular economy into product design, project operation, and business models.

  • Research

    We conduct field research through both qualitative and quantitative methods. Including observation, interviews, questionnaires, and data analysis, etc.

  • Project Design

    We help design, manage, or carry out projects in sustainability, community building and local development.

  • Education

    We provide workshops and courses on social entrepreneurship and circular economy to support education and raise public awareness of sustainability.

  • Creation

    We continually experiment with diverse approaches and innovative thinking to create and make changes. Including works that combine art, technology, and science.
