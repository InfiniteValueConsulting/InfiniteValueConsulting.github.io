Project

What we've worked on, our outcomes & impacts

Cultural Sustainability: Aesthetic Education in Rural Villages

Nov 2021, Qiandongnan, Guizhou

This project applies the methodology of cultural sustainability in the rural villages of Qiandongnan. We help analyze the plan and structure of the aesthetic education program. Through carrying out in-depth interviews, we understand the needs of rural education in these 4 local schools. In addition, we collected data and information on the minority's culture for further course design. Read More

WÚJIÈ Unlimited Space: Sustainable & Circular Economy Design

Oct 2021- Present, Wuyi Mt., Fujian

Together with "WÚJIÈ Unlimited Space" in Wuyi Mountain, we restore and redesign an old building with the idea of the circular economy. We apply the methodology of zero waste and sustainability in the use of space and items. We also help design events, creations, and projects related to sustainable development to increase public awareness. Read More

MAP OUT SUSTAINABILITY: What Does It Mean To Us?

Sep 2021, Beijing

This project combines technology and art. We use spatial analysis to map out the diversity and connections between the themes of sustainability. It is also a selected project to the “Local Knowledge and Ecological Perception of Diversity” workshop organized by the Gothe-Institute China. Read More

Organic Farm: Company Strategies & Education Program

Jul- Aug 2021, Shijiazhuang, Hebei

In this project, we help an organic farm analyze its current status and propose strategies for improvement and development. We also designed a children’s education program according to the demands of the organic farm to increase the number of visitors. Read More

Rural Community Building & Field Research Project

May- Jun 2021, Liancheng, Fujian
